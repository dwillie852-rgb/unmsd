Terms and operating rules

Clear boundaries for a high-risk medical operations site.

This website accepts leave relief requests into an operations review queue. The final organization name, legal entity, payment provider, data controller, and jurisdiction-specific terms must be confirmed before public launch.

No emergency medical request channel

The website must not be used to request urgent medical evacuation, emergency treatment, or security rescue. Live operations should publish verified emergency channels separately.

Leave relief approval

A leave relief request is not approved until the field medical lead, security desk, and operations desk confirm replacement coverage, movement safety, and handover readiness.

Fees and refunds

Leave relief estimates cover replacement staffing, handover, travel coordination, and an operations reserve. If coverage cannot be secured, unapplied programme fees should be refunded or reassigned only with the applicant's consent.

Payments

This local product tracks payment status in the admin dashboard. A public deployment should use a PCI-compliant payment provider, issue receipts, reconcile funds to operating lines, keep payment data out of the website codebase, and show applicants only limited status information through the public lookup.

Content accuracy

Programme statistics, field desk names, impact numbers, and operating timelines are placeholders until verified against real mission data and approved by leadership.

Brand status

The current name is a placeholder. Before launch, the final name should be checked for trademark, charity, domain, and public-confusion risk.
