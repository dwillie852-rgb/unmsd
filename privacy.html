Privacy notice

How personal information should be handled.

This prototype does not send form submissions to a server. Before launch, live intake must use secure storage, access controls, retention rules, and a named data controller.

Information we may collect

A live version of this site may collect contact details, relationship to a field doctor, field station, requested leave dates, donation enquiries, roster interest, and operational follow-up notes.

How it is used

Information should only be used to respond to enquiries, review leave relief requests, coordinate mission coverage, manage supporter communication, and meet legal or accounting obligations.

Sensitive data

Applicants should not submit patient names, medical records, security incident details, or identity documents through a public web form unless a secure upload and verification workflow has been implemented.

Retention

Production systems should define retention periods for enquiries, leave applications, payment records, roster submissions, consent logs, and audit trails. Data that is no longer needed should be deleted or anonymised.

Security controls required before launch

A production launch should include encrypted transport, protected admin access, role-based permissions, audit logs, spam controls, secure payment processing, backups, and a documented incident response contact.

Contact

Until the organization name and legal entity are final, privacy requests should be routed to the operations desk named in the final launch materials.
